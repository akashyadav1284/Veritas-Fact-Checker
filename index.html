Our Mission

Veritas was born out of a hackathon project with a single goal: to empower individuals with the tools to critically evaluate information. We believe that a well-informed public is the cornerstone of a healthy society. Our platform uses advanced algorithms to detect patterns commonly found in misinformation, helping you make more informed decisions about the content you consume and share.
